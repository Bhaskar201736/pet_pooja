FAQS

Will restaurant marketing help me gain more customers?

Yes! By marketing your menu, your food & your offers online, your old & potential customers will come to know about your restaurant. This will help bring more footfall to your outlet

What can I share on my restaurants social media account?

You can share menu updates, customer reviews, host giveaways & contests, & attract customers with special offers & discounts. You can explore unique templates from Marketing Hub & share them directly on your social media page.

Can I use Marketing Hub to get more orders on Zomato & Swiggy?

Yes. Your customers need to know what their order will look like. And so, Marketing Hub provides you with mouth-watering images for your online menu listings, which will help attract more orders

Can I customize Marketing Hub templates?

Yes, customising food templates in the Marketing Hub app is very easy. Select your favourite template, edit the graphic as you like, add your logo, and share it on your social media!
